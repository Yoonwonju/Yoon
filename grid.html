An X-ray machine is any machine that involves X-rays. It may consist of an X-ray generator and an X-ray detector. Examples include: Machines for medical projectional radiography Machines for computed tomography Backscatter X-ray machines, used as "body scanners" in airport security Detectors in X-ray astronomy In 1909, Mónico Sánchez Moreno inventor of the first portable x-ray machine.
