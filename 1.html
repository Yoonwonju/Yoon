Meaning of XrayMachine?

An X-ray machine is any machine that involves X-rays. It may consist of an X-ray generator and an X-ray detector. Examples include:
Machines for medical projectional radiography Machines for computed tomography Backscatter X-ray machines, used as "body scanners" in airport security Detectors in X-ray astronomy
